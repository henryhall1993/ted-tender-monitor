How it works

From raw procurement data to qualified opportunity

Three steps from published notice to actionable intelligence — delivered to your dashboard every Monday morning.

1
We monitor the sources
Every Monday our pipeline fetches hundreds of new procurement notices from TED (EU) and Find a Tender (UK) — the two official sources for European public tenders.
2
AI parses every notice
Our AI reads each notice — in whatever language it was written — and extracts a plain English summary, what they want, eligibility requirements, and evaluation criteria.
3
You see only what's relevant
Manufacturers see tenders matched to their product profile. Distributors see tenders in their territory. Both see partner matches. Everything ranked by relevance.
